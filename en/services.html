Consulting Services

I provide specialized consulting in the field of chemical safety, tailored to meet EU regulations and industrial needs. My services include:

1. Safety Data Sheets (SDS)

  • Creation of compliant SDS in accordance with EU/REACH regulations
  • Translation and localization into English, Czech, or Slovak
  • Regular updates and technical corrections to outdated SDS
  • Learn more

2. Chemical Audits

  • On-site or remote inspection of chemical safety practices
  • Storage, handling, and labeling compliance verification
  • Review of documentation and regulatory conformity

3. Employee Training

  • Customized safety training for teams working with hazardous materials
  • Sessions available in-person or remotely
  • Learn more

4. Chemical Safety Consulting & Risk Assessment

  • Identification of potential hazards in laboratory or industrial environments
  • Design and implementation of mitigation strategies
  • Development of clear and practical safety protocols
