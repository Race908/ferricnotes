Training

I offer customized training for employees and managers who work with or around hazardous substances. These sessions improve safety, compliance, and chemical awareness in your workplace.

Training Topics Include:

  • Proper handling and storage of hazardous chemicals
  • Understanding Safety Data Sheets (SDS)
  • Use of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
  • Labeling and identification of substances
  • Emergency response and spill protocols
  • REACH and CLP compliance overview

Training is available in-person or remotely, in Czech, Slovak, or English.

Contact me to discuss a custom training plan for your company.
