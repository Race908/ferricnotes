Services

I provide specialized consulting services in the field of chemical safety. My offerings include:

  • Safety Data Sheets (SDS) – Creation, translation, and update of SDS in compliance with EU/REACH regulations. More info
  • Chemical Audits – Comprehensive safety audits for chemical handling, storage, labeling, and documentation practices.
  • Employee Training – Hands-on or remote training sessions for staff working with hazardous chemicals. More info
  • Consulting & Risk Assessment – Hazard identification, risk mitigation strategies, and safety protocol development.

All services are available in Czech, Slovak, and English.
