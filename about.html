About Mgr. Rastislav Macko

I am a freelance consultant specializing in chemical safety, based in the Czech Republic. With a Master's degree in Physical Chemistry and years of industrial experience, I offer precise and compliant solutions in the fields of Safety Data Sheets (SDS), chemical audits, and employee training for hazardous substances.

I’ve worked with companies like Onsemi and U.S. Steel, handling material safety, regulatory documentation, and chemical risk analysis. I’ve also contributed to R&D projects and hold patents in the field of corrosion protection and process improvement.

Combining deep academic knowledge with real-world safety expertise, my mission is to make workplaces safer and chemical documentation clearer—across Czech, Slovak, and international clients.
